Curry Pasta Salad
1 (8 ounce) package uncooked tri-color spiral pasta 1 teaspoon curry powder salt to taste 1 tablespoon mayonnaise 1 tablespoon lemon juice 1 apple - peeled, cored and finely chopped ¼ cup raisins ¼ cup chopped green onions 2 carrots, grated ¼ cup firm tofu, crumbled 3 tablespoons pine nuts
