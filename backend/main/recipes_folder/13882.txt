Pineapple, Lime, and Ginger Soup
1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1 minced hot green chile peppers 2 cups water 1 cup crushed pineapple 1 tomato, diced (Optional) 1 ½ teaspoons salt 1 tablespoon fresh cilantro leaves 2 limes, juiced 1 tablespoon vegetable oil 1 ½ teaspoons cumin seeds
