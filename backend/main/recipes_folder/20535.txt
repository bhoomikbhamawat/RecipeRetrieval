Chicken, Fennel and Mushroom Soup
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 teaspoon butter 1 teaspoon lemon pepper 1 bulb fennel, trimmed and thinly sliced ¼ cup cream sherry 1 ½ cups sliced crimini mushrooms ¼ cup diced red bell pepper 3 tablespoons finely minced fresh parsley 1 cup buttermilk ½ cup half-and-half cream 1 ½ cups water 2 teaspoons chicken soup base
