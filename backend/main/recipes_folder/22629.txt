Asparagus, Chicken and Penne Pasta
1 (16 ounce) package dry penne pasta 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided ¾ pound skinless, boneless chicken breast meat - cut into bite-size pieces 4 cloves garlic, minced 12 ounces asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1 inch pieces 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes salt and pepper to taste ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
