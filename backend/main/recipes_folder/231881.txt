Citrus-Herb Vinaigrette
¼ cup red wine vinegar ¼ cup fresh orange juice 2 shallots, chopped 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice 2 tablespoons honey 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley 2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger 1 ½ tablespoons Dijon mustard 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice 1 tablespoon soy sauce 1 tablespoon fresh thyme 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped - or to taste 1 teaspoon chopped fresh dill 1 ½ cups grapeseed oil salt and ground black pepper to taste
