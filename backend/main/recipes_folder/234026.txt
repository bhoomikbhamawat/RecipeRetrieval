Canadian (French) Onion Soup
½ cup butter, divided 3 red onions, thinly sliced 5 cups water 2 beef bouillon cubes 1 chicken bouillon cube 1 (12 ounce) bottle Canadian beer (such as Steam Whistle Pilsner®) 1 garlic clove, crushed 1 bay leaf (Optional) freshly ground black pepper to taste 1 cup garlic croutons, or as needed 1 ½ cups shredded Italian three-cheese blend
