Hobo Ground Beef and Vegetable Soup
1 (32 fluid ounce) container beef broth, or more if needed 1 pound ground sirloin beef 1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained 1 (15 ounce) can green beans, drained 1 (15 ounce) can peas, drained 1 (14 ounce) can tomato sauce 3 carrots, cut into bite-size pieces 2 potatoes, peeled and cut into bite-size pieces 1 onion, chopped 1 large stalk celery, cut into bite-size pieces 1 clove garlic, minced 1 ½ tablespoons chopped fresh parsley ½ teaspoon celery seed 2 bay leaves salt and ground black pepper to taste
