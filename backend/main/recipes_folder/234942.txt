Almost Tuscan Sausage and Kale Soup
1 tablespoon olive oil, or as needed 1 large onion, chopped 2 cloves garlic, crushed 1 pinch red pepper flakes, or to taste 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste 1 pound bulk hot Italian sausage, removed from casings and crumbled ½ teaspoon chopped fresh basil ¼ teaspoon smoked paprika (Optional) 6 cups low-sodium chicken broth 4 cups chopped kale 1 (15 ounce) can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed 1 cup peeled and chopped yellow potatoes, or more to taste
