Marinated Tofu Salad with Capers
¼ cup olive oil 2 tablespoons white vinegar 1 teaspoon salt 1 teaspoon basil ½ teaspoon ground black pepper ½ teaspoon oregano 1 (12 ounce) package firm tofu, drained and cut into 1/2-inch cubes 2 tablespoons capers 1 head leafy dark green lettuce - torn, washed, and spun dry 10 cherry tomatoes, halved ¼ red onion, thinly sliced for garnish (or as preferred)
