Smoky Kale Salad
1 bunch green kale, stemmed and leaves roughly chopped 1 bunch red kale, stemmed and leaves roughly chopped ½ cup chopped pecans ¼ cup apple cider vinegar 1 tablespoon honey 1 ½ teaspoons chipotle pepper powder 1 ½ teaspoons smoked paprika 1 teaspoon liquid amino acid (such as Bragg®) 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and chopped
