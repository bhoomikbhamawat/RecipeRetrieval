Pineapple and Chicken Curry
6 slices bacon, sliced into small strips 1 onion, chopped 1 tart apple, chopped 1 ½ cups chicken broth 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained and juice reserved 1 (8 ounce) can pineapple rings, drained and juice reserved 2 tablespoons tomato puree 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1 ½ tablespoons curry powder salt to taste 5 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded to about 3/4-inch thickness
