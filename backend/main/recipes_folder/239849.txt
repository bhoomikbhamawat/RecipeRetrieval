Loaded Chicken Potato Soup
2 ½ pounds russet potatoes 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into chunks 2 tablespoons olive oil, or as needed 1 teaspoon salt, plus more to taste ½ teaspoon ground black pepper, plus more to taste 1 onion, diced 1 cup diced carrots 2 stalks celery, diced 6 tablespoons butter 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 3 cups milk 1 cup chicken stock 1 cup bite-size broccoli florets ½ cup bacon bits, divided 2 green onions, chopped 5 ounces shredded sharp Cheddar cheese, divided 1 cup sour cream
