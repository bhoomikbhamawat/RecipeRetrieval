Southern Style Chicken Perlo with Andouille Sausage
1 (3 pound) whole chicken 6 cups water, or as needed to cover 1 small onion, halved 1 bay leaf 1 sprig fresh thyme salt and pepper to taste 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 pound andouille sausage, cut into bite-size pieces 1 ½ cups uncooked long-grain white rice 1 teaspoon paprika 3 cups Swanson® Chicken Broth 2 hard-cooked eggs, peeled and chopped (Optional)
