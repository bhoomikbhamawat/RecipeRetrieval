Vegan Gluten-Free Mushroom Soup
1 tablespoon olive oil 1 large large onion, diced ¾ cup sliced fresh mushrooms 1 teaspoon dried thyme 3 cups vegetable broth 2 bay leaves 2 tablespoons gluten-free soy sauce (tamari) 6 tablespoons cornstarch ¼ cup cold water 3 cups coconut milk salt and ground black pepper to taste
