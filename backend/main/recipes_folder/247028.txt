Pork and Bamboo Shoot Soup with Cloud Ear
2 small dried cloud ear mushrooms 1 ½ cups water 4 ounces pork fillet, thinly sliced 4 ounces canned bamboo shoots, drained and chopped 2 tablespoons white sugar 2 tablespoons sake (Japanese rice wine) 2 tablespoons soy sauce 2 tablespoons black rice vinegar 1 teaspoon chile paste 3 eggs 1 ½ teaspoons sesame oil
