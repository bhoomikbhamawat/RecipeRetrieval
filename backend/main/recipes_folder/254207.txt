Succulent Sausage Squash Salad
1 small butternut squash - peeled, seeded, and cut into 1/2-inch cubes 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 teaspoon salt 4 ounces ground breakfast sausage 2 large apples, diced into 1/2-inch cubes 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves ¼ teaspoon ground sage ¼ teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg ½ cup balsamic vinegar ¼ cup maple syrup
