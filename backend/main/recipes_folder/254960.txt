Peanut Butter Goat Soup
5 cups water, divided 1 large onion, cut into thirds 2 tablespoons fresh ginger root ½ teaspoon chopped jalapeno pepper 2 pounds goat meat (chevon) 1 cube shrimp bouillon (such as Maggi®) (Optional) 1 ½ teaspoons salt ½ cup peanut butter 3 tablespoons tomato paste 2 leaves fresh basil (Optional)
