Brownie Batter Dip (aka Chocolate Hummus)
1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas (garbanzo beans), rinsed and drained ½ cup unsweetened dark cocoa powder ½ cup white sugar 3 tablespoons olive oil 3 tablespoons creamy natural peanut butter 1 teaspoon vanilla extract ½ teaspoon salt 3 tablespoons water, or more if needed
