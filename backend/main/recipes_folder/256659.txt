Creamy White Bean and Green Chile Soup
¼ cup butter ¼ cup all-purpose flour 3 ½ cups milk, or more to taste 1 (15 ounce) can white beans, drained 1 (4.5 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers ¼ teaspoon onion powder ¼ teaspoon ground cumin 1 pinch ground cayenne salt and ground black pepper to taste
