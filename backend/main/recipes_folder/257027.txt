Avocado Cucumber Pasta Salad
1 (16 ounce) package tri-color rotini pasta 2 tomatoes, seeded and diced 2 cucumbers - peeled, seeded, and diced 1 (4 ounce) can sliced black olives ½ cup Italian dressing, or more to taste ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste 1 avocado, diced 1 squeeze lemon juice
