Taiwanese Spicy Beef Noodle Soup
2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into 1-inch cubes water to cover 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, or more as needed 8 cups water, or more as needed 1 (14 ounce) can beef broth 1 cup soy sauce ½ cup rice wine 1 bunch green onions, cut into 2-inch pieces ¼ cup brown sugar 10 cloves garlic, peeled, or more to taste 4 small chile peppers, halved and seeded, or more to taste 2 tablespoons chile paste 1 (1 1/2 inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled and cut into 5 pieces 3 star anise pods, or more to taste 1 teaspoon Chinese five-spice powder 4 small heads baby bok choy 1 (10 ounce) package udon noodles 1 tablespoon chopped pickled Chinese mustard greens, or to taste (Optional)
