Asparagus Summer Soup
1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed and peeled 5 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided 5 cups chicken broth, divided 2 leeks, white and light green parts only, thinly sliced 1 pound fresh spinach, stemmed, divided 1 teaspoon white sugar 2 ½ tablespoons all-purpose flour ¼ teaspoon salt ⅛ teaspoon ground white pepper 1 cup frozen green peas ½ cup heavy whipping cream, or more to taste
