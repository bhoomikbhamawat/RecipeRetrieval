Confit of Leeks and Baby Carrots
2 tablespoons butter 5 leeks - white and light green parts sliced, separated into rings, and rinsed ¾ pound baby carrots with tops, peeled and trimmed ¼ cup dry white wine 1 ½ cups chicken stock 1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme 1 pinch white sugar 1 pinch freshly ground nutmeg 1 pinch salt to taste 1 pinch ground white pepper to taste
