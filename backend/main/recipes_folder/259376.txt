Cucumber Blueberry Feta Summer Salad
2 cucumbers - halved, seeded, and cut into 1/8-inch slices 2 cups blueberries 1 cup crumbled feta cheese ½ cup chopped scallions ¼ cup finely chopped flat-leaf parsley 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar 1 teaspoon honey salt and ground black pepper to taste
