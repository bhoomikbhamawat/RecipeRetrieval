Veggie and Wonton Soup
5 cups chicken broth 3 cups water ½ head napa cabbage, chopped 2 carrots - peeled, diced, or to taste 1 cup chopped snow peas 3 green onions, chopped 1 tablespoon fish oil 1 tablespoon sesame oil 1 (16 ounce) package frozen wontons (such as Trader Joe's® Chicken Gyoza Potstickers) 1 (8 ounce) container extra firm tofu, drained and diced ¼ cup miso broth concentrate 1 teaspoon salt 1 teaspoon sriracha hot sauce (Optional) ¼ teaspoon ground white pepper
