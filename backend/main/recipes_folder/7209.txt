Kalamata Olive and Garlic Bread
4 cups all-purpose flour ½ cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 1 tablespoon white sugar 1 tablespoon active dry yeast 1 tablespoon non-iodized salt ¾ cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 3 tablespoons olive oil 5 cloves garlic, minced ½ cup pitted Kalamata olives, chopped 1 tablespoon cornmeal
