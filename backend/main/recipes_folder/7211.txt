Cheese Herb Bread
1 ¼ cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 3 cups bread flour 2 tablespoons dry milk powder 2 tablespoons white sugar 1 ½ teaspoons salt 2 tablespoons butter, softened 3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese 1 ½ teaspoons dried marjoram 1 ½ teaspoons dried thyme 1 teaspoon dried basil 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
