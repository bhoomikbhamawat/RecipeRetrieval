Herbed Chicken and Fettuccini
9 ounces dry fettuccine pasta 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into strips 1 red onion, sliced 1 julienned yellow bell pepper 2 cloves garlic, minced ½ cup chicken broth 2 tablespoons sherry salt and pepper to taste 7 ½ ounces chive and onion cream cheese
