Mexican Chicken Corn Chowder
1 ½ pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size pieces ½ cup chopped onion 1 clove garlic, minced 3 tablespoons butter 2 cubes chicken bouillon 1 cup hot water ¾ teaspoon ground cumin 2 cups half-and-half cream 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese 1 (14.75 ounce) can cream-style corn 1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles 1 dash hot pepper sauce 1 tomato, chopped ¼ bunch fresh cilantro sprigs, for garnish
