Apple Walnut Salad with Cranberry Vinaigrette
½ cup chopped walnuts ¼ cup cranberries ¼ cup balsamic vinegar 1 cup red onion, chopped 1 tablespoon white sugar 1 tablespoon Dijon-style prepared mustard 1 cup vegetable oil salt and pepper to taste 10 cups mixed salad greens, rinsed and dried 2 Red Delicious apples, cored and thinly sliced
