Tomato Spinach and Basil Soup
2 tablespoons butter 1 large yellow onion, chopped 1 teaspoon minced garlic 1 ½ cups milk 1 (28 ounce) can tomato puree 1 tablespoon white sugar 2 cups fresh spinach, torn ¼ cup chopped fresh basil ½ teaspoon salt ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
