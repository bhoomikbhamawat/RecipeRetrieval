Tarragon Chicken and Rice Soup
4 cups water, or as needed 1 tablespoon chicken soup base, or to taste 1 tablespoon dried tarragon 2 teaspoons dried basil ground black pepper to taste 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves 1 cup long grain white rice 1 onion, diced 6 carrots, diced 6 stalks celery, diced 1 teaspoon finely chopped garlic
