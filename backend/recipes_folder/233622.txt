'Barry Good' Salmon Patty
4 pounds pink salmon (such as Chicken of the Sea®), drained, flaked and cartilage removed 4 large eggs 1 ½ cups panko bread crumbs ¾ cup mayonnaise 2 lemons, juiced 2 tablespoons ranch dressing mix 1 teaspoon salt 1 teaspoon ground black pepper 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1 teaspoon dried dill 1 teaspoon seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®) ½ cup white cornmeal ¼ cup vegetable oil, or as needed 16 lemon slices 16 sprigs fresh parsley
