Cranberry Apple Pecan Quinoa Salad
1 ½ cups chicken broth 1 cup quinoa, rinsed 3 tablespoons olive oil 1 ½ tablespoons Dijon mustard 1 teaspoon maple syrup, or more to taste ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon salt and ground black pepper to taste 1 large crisp apple, chopped into small pieces 1 cup pecan pieces ½ cup dried cranberries ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese (Optional)
