Balsamic Herbed Halibut
2 (6 ounce) halibut fillets 1 tablespoon seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®) ¾ cup balsamic vinegar 1 lemon, zested and juiced 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary ½ sweet onion, sliced 1 teaspoon oil, or as needed
