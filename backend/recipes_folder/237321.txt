Cherry Tomato Sauce with Penne
2 cups cherry tomatoes (such as Sun Gold) 2 tablespoons olive oil 2 cloves garlic, sliced salt and ground black pepper to taste 2 cups chicken broth 2 tablespoons fresh oregano leaves ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes 14 ounces penne pasta ½ cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
