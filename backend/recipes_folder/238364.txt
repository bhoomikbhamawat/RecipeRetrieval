Cilantro-Avocado Chimichurri Sauce
3 small avocados, flesh removed ½ white onion, cut into large chunks ½ bunch cilantro ½ bunch parsley 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded 1 lime, juiced 3 tablespoons white balsamic vinegar 3 cloves garlic, minced salt and ground black pepper to taste ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
