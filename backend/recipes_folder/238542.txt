7-Layer Salad
½ head lettuce, torn 1 cucumber, peeled and chopped, or more to taste 3 cups frozen peas, thawed 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese 4 hard-boiled eggs, peeled and sliced 1 cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip®) ½ cup white sugar 2 tablespoons white vinegar 12 ounces cooked crumbled bacon
