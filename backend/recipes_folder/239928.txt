Greek-Style Potatoes
1 ½ cups water ⅓ cup olive oil ¼ cup fresh lemon juice ¼ cup chopped fresh dill 2 cubes chicken bouillon, crushed 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped 1 teaspoon dried thyme 1 teaspoon dried rosemary 1 teaspoon dried oregano salt and ground black pepper to taste 6 potatoes, peeled and cut into inch-thick wedges
