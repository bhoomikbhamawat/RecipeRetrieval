Mom's Basic Vegetable Beef Stew
1 tablespoon olive oil, or as needed 2 onions, roughly chopped, divided 4 carrots, cut into 1/2-inch rounds 1 clove garlic, minced ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes ¼ teaspoon Greek seasoning (such as Cavender's®) (Optional) 1 pinch dried rosemary 1 pinch ground thyme 1 pinch dried basil 1 pinch Italian seasoning 1 bay leaf salt and ground black pepper to taste 1 (32 ounce) can diced tomatoes with juice 1 pound ground beef 4 potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks 1 stalk celery, chopped
