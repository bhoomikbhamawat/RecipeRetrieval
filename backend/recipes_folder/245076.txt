Thai Red Curry Hot Wings
3 pounds chicken wings, cut apart at joints, wing tips discarded 2 tablespoons cornstarch 1 ¾ cups Swanson® Chicken Stock 3 tablespoons red curry paste (such as Thai Kitchen®) 1 tablespoon sriracha hot chile sauce, or to taste 1 tablespoon brown sugar 1 teaspoon fish sauce (Optional) ¼ teaspoon garlic powder ¼ cup chopped cilantro (Optional)
