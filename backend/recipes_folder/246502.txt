Dak Dori Tang (Spicy Korean Chicken Stew)
¼ cup soy sauce 3 tablespoons gochujang (Korean hot pepper paste) 1 tablespoon brown sugar 1 tablespoon minced garlic 2 teaspoons sesame oil 1 ½ teaspoons minced fresh ginger root ½ teaspoon chile powder 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided, or as needed 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes 3 carrots, peeled and thickly sliced 2 potatoes, peeled and diced 1 leek, trimmed and thinly sliced 1 small onion, cut into 8 wedges 1 habanero pepper, seeded and minced 2 cups water, or as needed
