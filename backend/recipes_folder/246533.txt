Asparagus, Snow Pea, and Radish Salad
1 ¼ pounds asparagus, trimmed and cut diagonally into 1-inch pieces ½ pound snow peas, ends and strings removed, cut diagonally into 1-inch pieces 15 small radishes, trimmed and thinly sliced 3 scallions, thinly sliced ¼ cup fresh snipped dill 3 tablespoons canola oil 1 teaspoon white sugar ¾ teaspoon salt ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper 3 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice, or more to taste
