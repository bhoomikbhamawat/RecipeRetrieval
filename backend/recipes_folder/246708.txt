Cream of Thyme Asparagus Soup
1 tablespoon olive oil 2 small onions, chopped ½ cup chopped mushrooms, or to taste 1 teaspoon minced garlic, or to taste 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and coarsely chopped ⅓ cup dry white wine (Optional) 2 dashes dried thyme, or more to taste 1 dash ground black pepper 3 cups vegetable broth, or more to taste ½ cup heavy cream
