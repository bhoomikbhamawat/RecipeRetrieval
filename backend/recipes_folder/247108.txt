Paneer Tikka Sandwich
1 tablespoon vegetable oil 1 cup finely chopped onions 2 teaspoons red chile powder 1 teaspoon garam masala 1 teaspoon ground turmeric (haldi) 1 teaspoon white sugar salt to taste 2 cups cubed paneer 1 tablespoon tomato sauce 2 teaspoons butter, divided, or more to taste 2 slices bread
