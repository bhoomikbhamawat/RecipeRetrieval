Tomato, Cucumber and Red Onion Salad with Mint
2 large cucumbers - halved lengthwise, seeded and sliced ⅓ cup red wine vinegar 1 tablespoon white sugar 1 teaspoon salt 3 large tomatoes, seeded and coarsely chopped ⅔ cup coarsely chopped red onion ½ cup chopped fresh mint leaves 3 tablespoons olive oil salt and pepper to taste
