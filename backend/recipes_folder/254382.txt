Melanzane Ripiene con Capperi e Olive (Stuffed Eggplant with Capers and Olives)
9 small eggplants, halved lengthwise 2 eggs 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese 2 tablespoons capers 2 tablespoons pitted and sliced black olives 2 (16 ounce) cans tomato sauce, divided 1 bunch fresh parsley, chopped salt and ground black pepper to taste
