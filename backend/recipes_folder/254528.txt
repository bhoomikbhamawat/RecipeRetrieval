Hearty Pumpkin Beef Soup
12 ½ cups water 1 (1 pound) pumpkin, cut into cubes 2 pounds beef bottom round, cut into cubes ½ pound beef soup bones, trimmed of excess fat 2 tablespoons sea salt 1 (14 ounce) can MSG-free beef broth (such as Swanson®) 3 cloves garlic, minced 3 leaves Chinese thyme ground black pepper to taste 4 ears corn, cut into thirds 2 large carrots, chopped 1 large potato, cubed 1 (3 ounce) package pumpkin beef noodles (such as Maggi®) (Optional) 1 bitter melon, seeded and chopped 1 small radish, peeled and sliced into half moons 1 Scotch bonnet chile pepper, chopped 2 green onions, pressed flat with a knife
