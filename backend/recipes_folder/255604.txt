Baba's Best Sorrel Soup
½ cup unsalted butter 1 cup sliced onion 5 large cloves garlic, minced 12 cups tightly packed sorrel leaves, stems and veins removed 4 cups chicken stock 2 cups water 3 potatoes, peeled and diced 1 large carrot, peeled and cut into matchsticks 1 cup chopped fresh parsley 3 sprigs fresh dill, chopped, or to taste 1 tablespoon lemon juice 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper ¼ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper, or more to taste
