Green Tomato Soup
1 cup water, divided 1 bunch green onions (scallions), cut into pieces 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil 4 ½ pounds green tomatoes, diced 1 tablespoon chicken soup base 2 teaspoons salt, or to taste ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper ½ teaspoon granulated garlic ½ cup Chardonnay wine 1 pint light whipping cream 1 cup instant mashed potatoes (such as Idahoan® Buttery Homestyle®)
