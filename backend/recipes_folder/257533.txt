Capirotada a la Antigua (Mexican Bread Pudding)
1 guava - peeled, halved, and seeded 1 cup chopped piloncillo (Mexican brown sugar cones) peel of 1/2 orange 2 cinnamon sticks 5 whole cloves 5 whole allspice berries 1 ¾ cups water ¾ cup lard 1 baguette, cut into 1/2-inch slices ½ cup roasted peanuts ½ cup raisins ½ cup chopped pecans ½ cup candied pineapple (Optional) 3 ounces crumbled cotija cheese 1 tablespoon multi-colored sprinkles, or to taste
