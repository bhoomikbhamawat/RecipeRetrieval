Southwest Layered Salad
1 (1 ounce) package dry ranch dressing mix (such as Hidden Valley Ranch®) 1 cup buttermilk 1 cup fat-free plain yogurt ½ (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix, or more to taste 8 cups torn romaine lettuce 2 roasted red peppers, diced 1 (14 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained 1 (14 ounce) can white corn, drained ¼ cup finely chopped sweet onion 1 (4 ounce) can diced green chile peppers 1 cup diced Roma tomatoes ½ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese 1 large avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro (Optional)
