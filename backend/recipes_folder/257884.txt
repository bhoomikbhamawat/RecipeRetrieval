Okura and Sakura Shrimp Japanese-Style Spaghetti
1 (7 ounce) package spaghetti 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided 1 red bell pepper, seeded and chopped 8 pods okra, cut into bite-sized pieces 2 tablespoons soy sauce 1 tablespoon sake 1 tablespoon mirin (Japanese sweet rice wine) 2 ounces sakura (cherry) shrimp
