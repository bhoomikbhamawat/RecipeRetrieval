Snert (Dutch Split Pea Soup)
1 pound bone-in gammon (cured pork leg) 2 (14 ounce) bags dried split peas 8 ounces pork belly 1 small celeriac (celery root), peeled and chopped 2 leeks, sliced into 1/2-inch rings 2 small onions, chopped 1 carrot, chopped 1 smoked sausage 1 bunch fresh celery leaves, chopped salt and ground black pepper to taste
