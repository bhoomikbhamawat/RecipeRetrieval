Penne, Tomato, and Mozzarella Salad
1 (12 ounce) package penne pasta ¼ cup olive oil 1 bunch green onions, chopped 1 clove garlic, minced 1 cup quartered cherry tomatoes salt and pepper to taste 5 ounces mozzarella cheese, diced ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese 4 ounces fresh basil 12 large black olives, halved
