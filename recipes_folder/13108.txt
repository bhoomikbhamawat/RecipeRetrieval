Cream of Jerusalem Artichoke Soup
¼ cup butter 2 onions, minced 1 pound Jerusalem artichokes, roughly chopped 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed 1 tablespoon brandy 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour 3 cups chicken broth ¾ cup heavy whipping cream salt and pepper to taste ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley, for garnish
