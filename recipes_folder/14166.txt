Pasta Chickpea Salad
1 (16 ounce) package rotelle pasta 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil ½ cup chopped oil-cured olives 2 tablespoons minced fresh oregano 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley 1 bunch green onions, chopped 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans (chickpeas), drained and rinsed ¼ cup red wine vinegar ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese salt and pepper to taste
