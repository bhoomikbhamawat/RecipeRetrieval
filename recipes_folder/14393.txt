Tri-Mustard Salad
1 cucumber, scored and cut into 1/4 inch slices 1 green bell pepper, sliced 1 red onion, sliced 4 large tomatoes ¼ cup vegetable oil 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar 2 tablespoons Dijon-style prepared mustard 2 tablespoons mustard seed 1 teaspoon mustard powder ½ teaspoon hot pepper sauce
