Creamy Cauliflower Egg Salad
1 large head cauliflower, cut into small chunks 4 hard-cooked eggs, or more to taste - chopped 1 cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip®) 2 stalks celery, diced 4 green onions, chopped ¼ cup sour cream 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar 1 teaspoon white sugar salt and ground black pepper to taste
