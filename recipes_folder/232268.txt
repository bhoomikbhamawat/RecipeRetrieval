Easy and Delicious Ham and Potato Soup
3 ½ cups peeled and diced potatoes 1 cup diced cauliflower 1 cup frozen corn ¾ cup diced cooked ham ⅓ cup finely chopped onion ⅓ cup diced celery 3 cloves garlic, diced 4 cups water, or as needed to cover 2 tablespoons chicken bouillon granules ½ teaspoon salt, or to taste 1 teaspoon ground white pepper 5 tablespoons butter 5 tablespoons all-purpose flour 2 cups milk
