Turnip Green Soup
1 teaspoon vegetable oil 1 pound kielbasa sausage, cut into 1 inch pieces 1 onion, chopped 5 cups chicken broth 2 (20 ounce) cans turnip greens 2 (14.5 ounce) cans great Northern beans, rinsed and drained 1 package vegetable soup mix (such as Knorr's®) 1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce (such as Tabasco®) 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1 pinch Creole seasoning (such as Tony Chachere's®), or to taste salt and ground black pepper to taste
