Chef John's New Orleans-Style Barbequed Shrimp
1 ½ pounds colossal shrimp, EZ-peel type (deveined and shells split down the back) 1 ½ tablespoons vegetable oil 1 tablespoon freshly ground black pepper, or to taste ¼ teaspoon smoked paprika ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper ⅛ teaspoon seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®) (Optional) 1 tablespoon butter 2 cups chicken stock lemon, juiced 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce, or more to taste 2 dashes hot sauce, or to taste 3 tablespoons cold butter, cut into chunks 6 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon minced fresh rosemary 1 sprig fresh rosemary for garnish
