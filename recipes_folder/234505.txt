Spicy Southwest Corn Chowder
1 tablespoon vegetable oil 1 onion, chopped 2 jalapeno peppers, chopped 2 cloves garlic, chopped 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste 3 (15.25 ounce) cans whole kernel corn, drained 2 cups chicken broth 1 skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into small cubes 1 lime, cut into wedges (Optional) ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro (Optional) ¼ cup sour cream (Optional)
