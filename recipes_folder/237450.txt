Spicy Curried Sweet Potato Soup (Paleo and GF Approved)
3 sweet potatoes, peeled and chopped 2 cups beef broth 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk 1 small shallot, minced 2 tablespoons grade B maple syrup 2 teaspoons curry powder 1 teaspoon sea salt 1 teaspoon chili powder 1 teaspoon Spanish smoked paprika
