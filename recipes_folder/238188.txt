Summer Kale, Avocado, Mango, and Chickpea Salad with Citrus Poppy Seed Vinaigrette
6 cups shredded kale 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and cubed ½ mango - peeled, seeded, and cubed ⅓ cup chickpeas (garbanzo beans) ⅓ cucumber, thinly sliced 6 tablespoons olive oil 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar 1 tablespoon orange juice 1 tablespoon sunflower seed kernels, or to taste (Optional) 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard ½ teaspoon poppy seeds ½ teaspoon orange marmalade, or to taste (Optional)
