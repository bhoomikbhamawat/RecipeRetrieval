Moroccan Harira (Bean Soup)
6 cups beef stock1 cup dry lentils1 tablespoon olive oil, or to taste1 onion, chopped1 cinnamon stick1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root1 teaspoon ground turmeric1 teaspoon ground cumin1 teaspoon ground black pepper1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained1 (15 ounce) can red kidney beans, rinsed and drained1 (14 ounce) can diced tomatoes1 cup cooked quinoa (Optional)1 bunch flat-leaf parsley leaves and thinner stems, chopped1 bunch cilantro leaves and thinner stems, chopped1 lemon, or to taste, juiced
