Moroccan Harira (Bean Soup)
6 cups beef stock 1 cup dry lentils 1 tablespoon olive oil, or to taste 1 onion, chopped 1 cinnamon stick 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root 1 teaspoon ground turmeric 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1 teaspoon ground black pepper 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained 1 (15 ounce) can red kidney beans, rinsed and drained 1 (14 ounce) can diced tomatoes 1 cup cooked quinoa (Optional) 1 bunch flat-leaf parsley leaves and thinner stems, chopped 1 bunch cilantro leaves and thinner stems, chopped 1 lemon, or to taste, juiced
