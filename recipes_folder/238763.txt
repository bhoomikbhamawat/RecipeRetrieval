Sweet Potato Risotto from Reynolds®
1 Reynolds® Oven Bag, large size 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour 1 ½ cups uncooked Arborio rice 1 (8 ounce) sweet potato, peeled and shredded ½ cup chopped onion 2 tablespoons butter, softened and divided 2 teaspoons snipped fresh thyme 2 cloves garlic, minced ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper 3 cups reduced-sodium chicken broth ½ cup dry white wine or reduced-sodium chicken broth ¼ cup finely shredded Parmesan cheese, plus more for serving
