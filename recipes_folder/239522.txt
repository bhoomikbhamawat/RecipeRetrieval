Cauliflower Soup with Cheese
1 large head cauliflower, cut into florets and divided 1 ½ cups chopped onion ⅔ cup peeled and diced russet potato 1 carrot, peeled and chopped 3 cloves garlic 1 ½ teaspoons salt 4 cups water 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese ¾ cup milk 1 tablespoon minced fresh dill freshly ground black pepper to taste 1 teaspoon cumin seeds (Optional)
