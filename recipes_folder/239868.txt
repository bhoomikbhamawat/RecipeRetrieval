Spiced Apple Chutney
1 ½ cups white vinegar 1 ½ cups white sugar 4 tart apples - peeled, cored, and cut into 1/2-inch cubes ¼ cup diced dried apricots ¼ cup golden raisins ¼ cup diced shallots 5 thick slices fresh ginger ¼ teaspoon Aleppo pepper flakes, or to taste 1 whole star anise 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon kosher salt, or to taste ½ teaspoon yellow mustard seed
