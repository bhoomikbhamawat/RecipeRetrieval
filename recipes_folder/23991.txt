Gingery-Apricot Turkey Salad
2 cups diced, cooked turkey 1 Granny Smith apple - peeled, cored and diced 1 cup diced celery ½ cup chopped cashews ¼ cup dried apricots, finely chopped ½ cup low-fat mayonnaise ¼ cup low-fat sour cream 2 tablespoons apricot preserves ⅛ teaspoon ground nutmeg ¼ teaspoon ground ginger ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
