Ultimate Cod and Coconut Stew
2 tablespoons olive oil, divided, or as needed 2 pounds fresh cod, sliced into fillets salt and ground black pepper to taste 1 large sweet onion, sliced 1 large red bell pepper, sliced 4 large cloves garlic, minced 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk 1 green onion, chopped (green part only) 1 dash Sriracha hot sauce, or more to taste
