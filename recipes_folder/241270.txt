Edamame and Corn Salad
2 tomatoes, diced 1 cup shelled, cooked edamame 1 cup frozen sweet corn kernels, thawed 4 green onions, sliced 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro 2 cloves garlic, minced, or to taste 1 pinch coarse sea salt to taste (Optional)
