Tuna Salad with a Kick
2 (5 ounce) cans water-packed albacore tuna, drained and broken into chunks ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro ½ cup low-fat creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip Light®) ½ cup chopped red onion ½ cup chopped celery 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish 1 tablespoon prepared mustard, or to taste
