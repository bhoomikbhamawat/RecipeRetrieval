Savory Spanish Potato Salad
1 pound small red potatoes 3 tablespoons olive oil 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar 2 garlic cloves, crushed 1 small red bell pepper, chopped 2 tablespoons pimento-stuffed green olives, sliced 1 tablespoon minced shallot 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley salt and ground black pepper to taste 1 lemon, cut into wedges (Optional)
