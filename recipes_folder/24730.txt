Sun-Dried Tomato and Pesto Cheese Spread
4 cloves garlic, peeled 1 ½ cups fresh basil leaves 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice ¼ cup pine nuts 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil 2 ⅔ cups softened cream cheese ¼ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese 1 ⅓ cups sun-dried tomatoes, packed in oil, drained ⅓ cup tomato paste ¾ cup butter salt and pepper to taste
