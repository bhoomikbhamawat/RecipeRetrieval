Quinoa Salad with Roasted Yams
1 ½ cups diced yams 3 cups water 2 cups quinoa, soaked and rinsed 4 Persian cucumbers - peeled, trimmed, and cut into 1/4-inch cubes 1 cup chopped Italian parsley ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil ¼ cup 1/4-inch cubed onion 1 lemon, juiced 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar salt and ground black pepper to taste 5 endive spears to garnish (Optional)
