Chicken and Strawberry Risotto
2 tablespoons olive oil ½ onion, finely chopped 1 cup Arborio rice 2 ½ cups chicken stock ¾ cup white wine 10 strawberries, chopped, or more to taste 5 ½ ounces diced cooked chicken breast 6 tablespoons Parmesan cheese 2 tablespoons heavy whipping cream salt and ground black pepper to taste
