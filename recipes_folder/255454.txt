Chicken and Rice Taco Salad
2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite size pieces 1 tablespoon taco seasoning mix, or to taste 1 (5.4 ounce) package Knorr® Fiesta Sides™ - Yellow Rice 1 cup corn 1 cup no-salt-added black beans 1 (8 ounce) package shredded lettuce 1 medium tomato, diced 2 cups shredded reduced-fat Cheddar cheese 1 ½ cups crushed tortilla chips (Optional)
