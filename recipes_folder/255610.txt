Black Lentil Veggie Soup
1 ½ cups black lentils 2 quarts vegetable broth 3 cups diced tomatoes 10 carrots, peeled and diced 1 onion, cut into 1/2-inch dice 2 small turnips, peeled and diced 1 green bell pepper, diced 3 tablespoons unsalted butter 2 tablespoons minced ginger 2 cloves garlic, minced ½ teaspoon ground coriander ½ teaspoon ground cumin salt to taste
