Potato Leek Soup (Vichyssoise)
4 tablespoons unsalted butter 1 ½ pounds trimmed leeks, cleaned and thinly cut 2 teaspoons kosher salt, plus more to taste 1 ¼ pounds Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled, cut into eighths 3 cups chicken or vegetable broth, or more as needed to adjust thickness 1 cup heavy cream ½ cup creme fraiche 1 pinch cayenne pepper Chopped fresh chives for garnish
