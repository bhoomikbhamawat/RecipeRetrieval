Layered Waldorf Salad
1 cup shelled walnut pieces 1 large head green leaf lettuce, sliced into bite-size chunks 3 cups chopped celery 1 pound red seedless grapes, halved 2 large red apples, cut into small chunks ⅓ cup blue cheese crumbles ¾ cup plain yogurt 3 tablespoons mayonnaise 1 teaspoon honey 1 teaspoon lemon juice 1 teaspoon lemon zest salt and ground black pepper to taste
