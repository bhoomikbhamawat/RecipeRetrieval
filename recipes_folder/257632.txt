Cauliflower Clam Chowder (Low Calorie)
1 tablespoon olive oil 1 onion, sliced 5 cups water 2 cups chopped carrots 1 (16 ounce) package frozen cauliflower florets 4 teaspoons chicken bouillon granules 1 clove garlic, minced 2 (10 ounce) cans whole baby clams 2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms salt and ground black pepper to taste 1 pinch red pepper flakes (Optional)
