Bacon-Wrapped Potatoes with Honey-Scallion Sauce
2 large potatoes, cut into 4 to 6 wedges 8 slices bacon, or more as needed 8 toothpicks 2 cups canola oil, or as needed 2 tablespoons butter, melted 1 tablespoon honey, or to taste 1 tablespoon chopped green onion ½ teaspoon chopped fresh parsley ¼ teaspoon salt ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
