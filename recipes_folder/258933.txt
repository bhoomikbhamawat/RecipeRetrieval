Mediterranean Zucchini 'Pasta' Salad
1 (10 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved ½ cup pitted Kalamata olives, halved 3 zucchini, spiralized 2 lemons, juiced ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil 2 tablespoons chopped parsley 1 tablespoon white vinegar 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 teaspoons dried oregano 1 teaspoon kosher salt 1 lemon, zested ½ teaspoon ground black pepper ¼ cup crumbled goat milk feta cheese, or to taste
