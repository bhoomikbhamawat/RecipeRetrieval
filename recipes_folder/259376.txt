Cucumber Blueberry Feta Summer Salad
2 cucumbers - halved, seeded, and cut into 1/8-inch slices2 cups blueberries1 cup crumbled feta cheese½ cup chopped scallions¼ cup finely chopped flat-leaf parsley3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil2 tablespoons white wine vinegar1 teaspoon honeysalt and ground black pepper to taste
